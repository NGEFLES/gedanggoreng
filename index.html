Gedang Goreng are a snack that is often found in Indonesia , Malaysia and Singapore . This food is made from bananas that have been peeled. Bananas are cut into pieces according to your wishes then coated with a thick mixture made from a mixture of flour, a little salt and sugar, then fried in hot oil.
